# FAT Agent Suite Vision

## Product Statement

FAT Agent Suite is a Codex Desktop-native copilot for a single-entity Finance, Accounting, and Tax team. It reads company context, follows internal policy, and helps the team run monthly close, answer SOP questions, review draft work, and prepare reusable working artifacts.

## Users

- Finance staff handling cash, payment preparation, AP/AR follow-up, and budget monitoring.
- Accounting staff handling journals, reconciliations, close adjustments, and supporting schedules.
- Tax staff handling treatment review, compliance checklists, and working papers.
- Reporting preparers or reviewers handling commentary, variance analysis, and package consistency checks.

## Core Value

- Reduce repetitive interpretation of policy and COA.
- Turn close checklist and templates into an operational workflow.
- Improve consistency and review quality before human approval.
- Preserve an auditable trail through file-based artifacts.

## Top Use Cases

1. Ask how a transaction should be treated under policy and COA.
2. Route a user request to finance, accounting, tax, or reporting.
3. Review a draft reconciliation and surface missing evidence.
4. Review a draft journal and suggest treatment or escalation.
5. Prepare a monthly close action list from the checklist and current status.
6. Check required tax support for a recurring transaction type.
7. Draft tax working paper notes from policy and transaction evidence.
8. Draft variance commentary for monthly management reporting.
9. Flag inconsistency between schedules, TB, and reporting output.
10. Prepare issue logs and blockers during period close.

## Success Criteria

- Team can start from one coordinator agent.
- Agents answer common SOP questions using company documents.
- Review outputs are structured and reusable.
- Period close status is visible through workspace files.
- No agent performs unsafe autonomous actions.
