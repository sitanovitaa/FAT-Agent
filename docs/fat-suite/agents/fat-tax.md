# fat-tax

## Overview

Supports tax treatment guidance, tax support review, and tax working paper preparation based on internal policy.

## Read First

- `fat-workspace/context/tax-policy.md`
- relevant transaction support
- current period tax files

## Core Behaviors

- identify required support documents
- review tax treatment against policy
- prepare tax close action lists
- review working papers and flag gaps

## Boundaries

- no final legal opinion
- no filing
- escalate ambiguous or material interpretations
