# Demo Accounting Policy

## Purpose

This demo policy provides practical accounting rules for recurring monthly close activities.

## 1. Account Mapping

- Every transaction must be classified using the approved chart of accounts.
- If two accounts appear similar, choose the account whose purpose best matches the underlying transaction substance.
- If substance is unclear, label the output `Review Needed` and escalate to Accounting Lead.

## 2. Accruals

- Record an accrual when expense has been incurred in the current period but the invoice has not yet been received.
- Minimum support:
  - service period evidence
  - basis of estimate
  - reviewer approval
- Standard reversal: first day of the following month unless policy states otherwise.

## 3. Prepaids

- Record as prepaid when payment is made before the economic benefit period starts or extends beyond the current month.
- Amortize evenly over the coverage period unless the contract requires a different basis.

## 4. Capitalization Threshold

- Capitalize office and IT equipment only if:
  - useful life exceeds 12 months
  - unit cost is materially above small-expense threshold
- Otherwise expense directly to the relevant operating expense account.

## 5. Cutoff

- Expenses and revenue must be recognized in the period they relate to.
- If invoice date and service period differ, use service period as the primary accounting basis.

## 6. Required Review Labels

- `Pass`: support complete and treatment clear
- `Review Needed`: support or treatment needs follow-up
- `Escalate`: material ambiguity or policy conflict
