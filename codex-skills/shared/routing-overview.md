# FAT Routing Overview

- `fat-accounting`: accounts, journals, accruals, prepaids, reconciliations, close adjustments
- `fat-tax`: VAT or PPN, withholding, tax treatment, tax support, tax working papers
- `fat-finance`: payments, cashflow, AP or AR follow-up, budget monitoring
- `fat-reporting`: variance commentary, management pack summaries, consistency checks
- `fat-coordinator`: close status, blockers, next-step planning, ambiguous or cross-functional requests

When ambiguous, route through `fat-coordinator` first.
